Summer is already here and Águilas shines with its own light. Our beaches, the Castle of San Juan de las Águilas, the promenades, and the local gastronomy are more than enough reasons to visit us. But, let's be honest, there comes a point in the holidays when your body asks for a bit of action and a different plan than just roasting on your towel.

If you are on vacation on our coast and are looking for an original activity, **pickleball** at Isla del Fraile is exactly what you need.

## The star sport of the summer (and of your vacation)

Why has pickleball become the favorite leisure plan for tourists who visit us? 

1. **It is for the WHOLE family:** Few sports allow parents, teenage children, and even grandparents to play together on the same court, having just as much fun. Since the court size is reduced and the paddle is very light, brute force is useless; it's all a matter of skill and reflexes.
2. **Zero frustration:** If you go on vacation, you don't want to get stressed. Unlike tennis or padel, which require more initial technique, in pickleball you will be rallying and laughing out loud within 10 minutes.
3. **Sporty sunsets:** In July and August, playing in the blazing sun is not the most appealing idea. Our illuminated courts in the exclusive Isla del Fraile resort are the perfect plan for 8:00 PM or 9:00 PM. A good match as the sun goes down and dinner afterwards. Sounds good, right?

## "But I'm here as a tourist and I don't have the equipment"

No problem! We know that in your holiday suitcase you have enough to deal with packing your swimsuit, sunscreen, and flip-flops.

At **Pickleball Águilas Resort** we hand it to you on a silver platter. When you book your court, you can request the **rental of all the necessary equipment**. We give you the paddles and balls, you just have to provide the sports clothes and the desire to have fun.

## Put the icing on your summer

Don't let your vacation in Águilas be "more of the same". A few games of pickleball will give your summer that sporty and different touch that is always remembered upon your return.
