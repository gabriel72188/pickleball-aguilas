The lights, company dinners, holiday sweets, and... the eternal question are already here: *What do I give this year that isn't another pair of pajamas or the typical bottle of cologne?* If you are looking for an original gift for that special someone, a family member, or even for yourself, at **Pickleball Águilas Club** we have a proposal that never fails: **gifting an experience**. And we assure you that gifting a few hours of pickleball means gifting health, laughter, and disconnection.

## The gift nobody expects (and everybody enjoys)

We increasingly value experiences over material things. Inviting someone to discover pickleball on our Isla del Fraile courts is the perfect gift for several reasons:

1. **It is suitable for everyone:** It doesn't matter if the person you are giving it to is 20 or 65 years old, or if they are an elite athlete or haven't stepped on a court in years. Pickleball adapts to any level from the very first minute.
2. **It's a plan to do together:** Gifting a pickleball match means gifting quality time. You can come as a couple, invite another, and set up a hilarious doubles match to work off those holiday feasts.
3. **We provide the equipment:** You don't need to buy paddles as a gift (unless you want to). We rent out all the necessary equipment. 

## The best New Year's resolution

January always comes loaded with good intentions: eating better, quitting smoking, joining the gym... The problem with the gym is that, for many, it ends up being boring and gets abandoned by February.

Why not change the goal this year? Set out to do sports in a way where you don't even realize you are training. Coming to play pickleball a couple of times a week or signing up for our [Classes](/en/servicios) is the best way to fulfill that "getting in shape" resolution while socializing and laughing out loud.

## How to organize it?

If you want to prepare a special gift or book your court to start the month of January with energy, drop by our [Contact](/en/contacto) section or write to us directly.
