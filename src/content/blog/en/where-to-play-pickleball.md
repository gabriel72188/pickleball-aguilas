Pickleball in Águilas (Murcia) has become one of the most popular sport activities for both residents and visitors alike.

## Why play pickleball in Águilas?

Águilas offers ideal conditions for practicing pickleball:
- More than 300 sunny days a year.
- First-class facilities at Isla del Fraile.
- A welcoming atmosphere for players of all levels.

## Pickleball for beginners

One of the great advantages of pickleball is how easy it is to learn. Beginners can start playing from day one with no prior experience required.
