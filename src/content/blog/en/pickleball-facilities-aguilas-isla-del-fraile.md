When you decide to practice a sport, the place where you do it matters just as much as the exercise itself. At **Pickleball Águilas**, we wanted to go beyond offering simple courts; we have created a space where physical activity merges with one of the most emblematic landscapes of the Murcian coast: **Isla del Fraile**.

Today we invite you to take a virtual tour of our facilities and discover why we are the favorite meeting point for "picklers" in the region.

## An Environment That Inspires

Located in the heart of the exclusive area of **Isla del Fraile in Águilas**, our courts enjoy an elevated position that offers spectacular views of the Mediterranean.

Playing here means enjoying the sea breeze, which regulates the temperature even on the hottest summer days. It is, undoubtedly, the perfect microclimate for outdoor sports.

## Professional-Level Courts

We haven't skimped on details so that your game is fluid and safe:
- **Next-generation surface:** Our courts feature a specific coating that guarantees an even ball bounce and optimal grip to prevent slipping.
- **LED Lighting:** Prefer to play as the evening sets in? Our high-intensity lighting is designed to eliminate annoying shadows, allowing you to see the ball with total clarity on every point.
- **Daily Maintenance:** We ensure that every net and line is in pristine condition so you only have to worry about having fun.

## Much More Than Just Courts

We know that Pickleball is, above all, a social sport. That's why our facilities are designed for the "before" and "after" of the match:

* **Equipment Rental:** If you don't have your own paddle or want to try the latest graphite models, our reception offers a high-end equipment rental service.
* **Rest Area:** A space to recover your strength, discuss the best plays with your rivals, and enjoy the surroundings.
* **Community:** We organize meetups ("Open Plays") where you can come alone and we will find you a partner and opponents of your same level.

## Easy Access and Convenience

Forget about driving around looking for parking. We have **free parking** for all our users, ensuring your visit is stress-free from the moment you arrive.

Whether you're an Águilas resident looking for your weekly sports routine or a visitor who wants to discover **Isla del Fraile** through sports, our doors are open to you.
