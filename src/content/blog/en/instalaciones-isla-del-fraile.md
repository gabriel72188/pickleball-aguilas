With the arrival of June, the days are longer, the sky is bluer, and the desire to be outdoors multiplies. But when the heat starts to show up in Águilas, the place you choose to play sports makes the difference between suffering and enjoying.

And that is where **Pickleball Águilas Resort** has the advantage. We are not in an industrial estate or surrounded by asphalt; we are lucky to be located in the spectacular **Isla del Fraile**.

## Much more than just courts

When you come to play pickleball with us, the experience starts the moment you cross the resort's entrance. Isla del Fraile is synonymous with tranquility, nature, and exclusivity. 

* **The perfect setting:** Playing surrounded by palm trees, the sea breeze, and a meticulously cared-for environment makes the disconnection from daily stress complete. 
* **Top-level courts:** We have prepared our courts with high-quality materials that guarantee a perfect ball bounce and protect your joints, reducing the risk of injuries.
* **No crowds:** Being in an exclusive area, you free yourself from the constant noise of traffic and the hustle and bustle of the center. Here you only hear the sound of the birds and the unmistakable *pop* of the paddle hitting the ball.

## The famous "Third Half"

If you have already read about us on the [About Us](/en/quienes-somos) page, you will know that Jose and Carla are big fans of what happens *after* the match. 

The facilities at Isla del Fraile are ideal for that post-match moment. Finishing a good pickleball session and being able to sit down for a cold drink, discuss the best plays with friends, and enjoy the Águilas sunset in a premium setting... that is priceless.

## Late afternoon matches? Yes, please

As June progresses, playing at 4 in the afternoon is no longer a good idea. Our courts have perfect lighting so you can book your time at sunset or at night, enjoying the ideal temperature of the Murcian coast.

Whether for a class or a free match, we assure you that you will fall in love with the setting as much as the sport. Get your paddle ready and come discover the best-kept secret of pickleball in Águilas!
