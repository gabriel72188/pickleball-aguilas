The weekend arrives, the weather is spectacular in Águilas, and the eternal question arises: *What are we doing today that the whole family will like?* Going for a walk is great, but sometimes the body asks for a bit of action, laughter, and a break from the routine.

If you are looking for an activity where everyone from the little ones to the grandparents can participate, **pickleball** at Isla del Fraile is the perfect plan.

## The most intergenerational sport in the world

In most sports, physical differences in age or height make it impossible for a 10-year-old child to play a balanced match against an adult. However, pickleball has "magic" in this sense:

1. **The court is small:** You don't need a great physical background or to make huge sprints to reach the ball.
2. **The paddle and ball are very light:** Kids don't get tired when hitting and there's no risk of getting hurt, and for the older ones, it's a very low-impact sport for the joints.
3. **Pure strategy:** Here, the winner isn't the one who hits the ball the hardest, but the one who knows how to place it. You'd be surprised to see the kids beating their parents based on reflexes at the net!

## A different weekend

Booking a court at **Pickleball Águilas Resort** to spend the morning or afternoon with the family guarantees laughter, anecdotes, and super healthy exercise without you even noticing.
