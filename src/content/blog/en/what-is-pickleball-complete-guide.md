## What exactly is Pickleball?

Pickleball is a paddle sport that combines elements of three very popular disciplines: **tennis, badminton, and ping-pong (table tennis)**.

It is played on a court with the same dimensions as a doubles badminton court, with a center net similar to a tennis net (but slightly lower). To play, solid paddles are used (usually made of fiberglass or carbon) along with a hollow plastic ball with holes, which creates a slower and more predictable flight.

## Why is it the trendiest sport?

The overwhelming success of pickleball is no accident. Here are the keys that have made it the favorite sport of thousands of people:

* **Extremely fast learning curve:** Unlike tennis or padel, which require months of technique to achieve fluid rallies, in pickleball **you can be playing and having fun from day one**, even if you've never picked up a racket.
* **Less impact, more health:** Being a smaller court, it requires less running and the movements are less abrupt on the knees and joints. It's perfect for staying in shape at any age.
* **Extremely social:** It's a sport played mostly in doubles format. The proximity on the court facilitates conversation, laughter, and the creation of a fantastic community.
