## The health benefits of Pickleball

Pickleball isn't just fun and laughter (although it's that too); it's a fantastic cardiovascular exercise that brings many benefits to your body and mind:

1. **Burn calories without realizing it:** A one-hour match at a good pace will make you sweat and burn calories almost without noticing it, because you'll be too focused on getting the ball over the net and winning the point.
2. **Protect your joints:** Unlike running on asphalt or playing on huge courts, pickleball has a much lower impact on your knees and ankles. The movements are shorter and more controlled, making it an ideal sport to keep you agile at any age.
3. **Mental health and goodbye stress:** Going back to work generates stress, it's inevitable. Hitting the ball, moving outdoors in our Isla del Fraile resort, and socializing with other players is the best therapy to disconnect your head from meetings and emails.
