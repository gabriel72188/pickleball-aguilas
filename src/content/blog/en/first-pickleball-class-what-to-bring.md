You've read about the sport, watched a couple of videos online, and finally decided to **play pickleball in Águilas**. You have your reservation at our **Isla del Fraile** courts, but suddenly a doubt arises: *"Now what? What do I wear? Do I have to bring anything special?"*.

It's normal to have these doubts on your first day. To ensure you arrive at the court with complete peace of mind and only worry about enjoying yourself, we have prepared this quick guide for your first visit.

## 1. Clothing: Comfort Above All

You do not need professional-level equipment or exclusive clothing. The dress code in pickleball is very relaxed:
* **Shorts or athletic leggings:** Any garment that allows you to bend your knees and move laterally with comfort. Shorts with pockets are ideal for holding an extra ball.
* **Breathable shirt:** We play outdoors under the Águilas sun, so a technical fabric t-shirt or light cotton will be your best ally.
* **Cap and sunglasses:** Essential for playing comfortably, especially for morning or midday matches.

## 2. Footwear: The Most Important Detail

If you're going to invest in one thing before coming, let it be footwear. **Avoid running shoes**. Running shoes are made for moving forward and have a high sole that can cause sprains if you make sudden lateral movements.
* **The ideal:** Tennis shoes, padel shoes, or specific indoor court shoes. They have a flatter sole and lateral reinforcements that will give you the necessary stability.

## 3. What do I pack in my bag?

Your packing list for coming to Isla del Fraile should be simple:
* **Plenty of water:** Staying hydrated is vital, points are fast and you will warm up right away.
* **Sunscreen:** Don't let the sea breeze fool you!
* **A small towel:** To dry your sweat between games.

**What about the paddle and balls?**
Don't worry about that! If it's your first time, we have a top-quality equipment rental service at our reception. Just let us know when you arrive and we will give you the right paddle for your level.

## 4. What will the atmosphere be like?

This is the favorite part for our new players. Pickleball is world-famous for being the most welcoming sport in existence.

When you arrive at **Pickleball Águilas**, you won't find an elitist or hyper-competitive atmosphere. You will find an open community, laughter in the background, and veteran players who will be happy to give you two or three quick tips before starting. Respect and fun are placed above pure competition.

**All set?**
Now you know everything you need for your debut. Relax, come ready to move, and prepare to hear the addictive sound of the paddle hitting the ball. See you on the court!
